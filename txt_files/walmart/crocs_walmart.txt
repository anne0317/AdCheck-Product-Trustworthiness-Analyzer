Size covers 2 US sizes
The US size is stamped on the bottom of shoe and the tags state sizing in EU, so the sizing is European converted to US. For example I wear women's US 11 to 11.5, and that's European size EU 42-43. EU sizing is gender neutral, so their 42 - 43 size applies for women US 11-12 AND men US 8-9. Because these Crocs are made to cover 2 sizes for either gender, they may run a little large or small depending where your US size is in the range. Mine have about an inch of space so I might have been able to go down a size but then they might be tight and I couldn't wear socks. So consider how tight you like the fit and whether you want to wear socks.
