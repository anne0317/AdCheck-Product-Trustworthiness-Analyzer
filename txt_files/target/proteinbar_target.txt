Underwhelmed
I'm a HUGE fan of vanilla just about everything. These bars don't really taste a whole lot like vanilla. They definitely don't smell like vanilla very much & my box full was delivered warm (it's already into the 80s in my area) so I was really looking forward to a very vanilla bar. Shipping took nearly 10 days! To wait that long wasn't worth all the hype surrounding the bar & I wonder the validity to the reviews. Again, I'm a huge lover of just about all things vanilla & while these bars DO taste good, they're not great. They will be good for my work in the construction field as there isn't any chocolate in these to melt all over the package & eventually make a mess on me as I try to eat a few bites at a tome in between working. These bars seem to have a very salty aftertaste like the dark chocolate sea salt does, possibly covering for a lack of actual flavor. For all the hype, I'm definitely disappointed in the underwhelming vanilla flavor. I may or may not buy these again but if I do, it will largely be due to no chocolate in the bar so no mess. Definitely won't be because I loved the flavor. The bar does tend to have more whole almonds in this one vs others which tend to have pieces or halves. I do like that & they have a really smooth, much less stick to your teeth texture than the other bars which I really like. It's almost like a fluffy texture. 1 star deduction for underwhelming flavor. I'd love to see more flavors without chocolate. I know, blasphemy.
