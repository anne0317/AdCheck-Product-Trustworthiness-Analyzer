A Mini with potential!
I purchased my first Mini in August 2018. It lasted until December of that year before it started having issues with the brewing; as many other reviewers have noted, it would only drain a few ounces of the water, then stop. Sometimes, I could unplug it for a minute or two, lift and press down the lever and get it to work, but it was happening with alarming regularity and that’s annoying every time I wanted a cup of coffee. In January of 2019, I purchased a new Mini (at what I consider to be a great price of just sixty dollars) and while it doesn’t happen nearly as often as it did with my first Mini, I still occasionally have issues with the water not draining for the brewing cycle. Because we have well water (with a softener), I use filtered water from my refrigerator in the Keurig. I have found that running a vinegar & water solution through it to descale once every 3-4 months does seem to help. Another odd thing that seems to help is that I unplug my Mini after every use. As of now, I might have an issue with the water draining maybe once a month, but because our kitchen is small and the Mini takes up so little space, I’m willing to deal with the problem to get a fresh, hot cup of coffee (almost) whenever I want it. So, if you can find it on sale and you’re willing to work with it a little, it can be a great little single serve coffee maker!
