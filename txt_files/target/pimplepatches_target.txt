Favorite patches--and I've tried a bunch
When used correctly, these significantly accelerate healing. It’s a misconception that hydrocolloidal patches suck out gunk. Covering a spot with a patch really just promotes an anaerobic healing environment, keeping the area moist, clean, and covered which in itself promotes better healing and lessens likelihood of bad scarring. The white that appears on the patch is not necessarily “gunk” that’s been sucked out, it’s mostly moisture that the spot has secreted while undergoing the healing process. I typically use these on freshly popped/picked pimples as I’d use a BandAid on a wound, but they also work on whiteheads. For whiteheads, they shrink the breakout and reduce swelling and redness (but again, they don’t really extract gunk). I have used them on deeper cystic acne (doesn’t have extractable gunk) as well to, again, promote a healing environment. The patches are still white in the morning (from moisture, not gunk) and the spot is less red and flatter. Blackheads and closed comedones won’t look much different in the morning and the patch won’t look white when you remove it. The white signifies that the healing process has happened so if you don’t see white, the patch wasn’t used on the right type of spot. I use these on specific spots at night after double cleansing (cleansing balm then gel cleanser), applying hyaluronic acid serum, and applying rx clindamycin gel and rx Tretinoin/niacinamide combo cream to my entire face. I let products dry down and them apply the patches and moisturize around them. I’ve tried a bunch of brands of patches (COSRX, Rael, Starface, Truly, PanOxyl, Peace Out) and these are the thickest and stickiest (they will last over 24hrs). When you remove them, the spot looks less angry and moisturized (they help keep things from getting really scabby/crusty). The longer you can leave them on, the better and more healed the problem spot will look when you remove them. For larger areas of the face (picked areas, cystic areas) I really recommend the large size BandAid brand Hydro Seal bandages. They are just jumbo hydrocolloidal patches that work the exact same way. A popped pimple is a wound and covering wounds (not letting them “air out” like your mom told you to do when you were a kid) is the best, fastest way to let your body heal.
